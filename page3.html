Llamas have three stomachs!


It's the goldilocks of 
stomach numbers, at least
that's probably how they feel.

If you put too much weight on a llama,
it will refuse to move.


Llamas may also spit, hiss, or even kick at 
their owners until their burden is lessened


Llamas can carry 50 pounds for 20 miles in one day


A llama is a useful friend! That said, you'll be hard-pressed to get an Alpaca to do the same thing, as their bone structure can't handle much more than their own body weight.

Llama poop is sellable.


Llama Brew is a LA-based company that sells llama poop as an all-natural fertilizer. Their tagline: "What makes Llama Brew? Llama's poo!"
